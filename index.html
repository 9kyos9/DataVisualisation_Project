Datasets

A single data source (provided on KEATS) is used for the visualisations.

  • Summer Olympics Medals (1896-2024): https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/stefanydeoliveira/summer-olympics-medals-1896-2024?resource=download

Simple Guide for Visualisations

  • Choropleth Map
    • By Controlling the slider above the map, the map will be updated to the information of the year.
  • Racing Bar Chart
    There are two ways to interact with this visualization:
    • Manually adjusting the slider
    • Watching the chart animate automatically using the play button next to the slider.
